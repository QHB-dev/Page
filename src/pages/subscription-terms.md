## 4. How to Cancel

Your cancellation path depends on where you originally purchased or started your trial. Please follow the instructions that match your purchase channel.

### 4.1. If you subscribed on our website

You can cancel online, at any time, in the shedio web app: go to https://shedio.app, sign in, open Profile → Subscriptions, and tap "Turn off auto-renewal." Cancelling online in the shedio web app is at least as easy as subscribing, and we will not require you to call, mail, or contact a live agent in order to cancel.

Canceling your subscription disables automatic renewal. You will continue to have access to all subscription features for the remainder of your then-current paid period.



### 4.2. If you subscribed on the Apple App Store

You can cancel at any time through your Apple ID subscription settings. Only you can manage subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store — we cannot cancel them for you.

- On iPhone or iPad: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → shedio → Cancel Subscription.
- See: https://support.apple.com/HT202039
To avoid being charged, cancel at least 24 hours before the end of your trial or current subscription period.



### 4.3. If you subscribed on Google Play

You can cancel at any time through your Google Play subscription settings.

- On Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → shedio → Cancel subscription.
- See: https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/7018481
To avoid being charged, cancel at least 24 hours before the end of your trial or current subscription period.



### 4.4. What happens after you cancel

- You keep access to all paid features for the remainder of your then-current paid period.
- No new charges will be made on your payment method after cancellation, unless you restart your subscription.
- Your data stays with you for the duration of your account. You can delete your account at any time — see our Privacy Policy.
- Resubscribing is always possible — your progress and preferences will be available if your account still exists.
We will not retain you against your will. During cancellation we may offer you alternatives such as pausing your subscription, switching to a cheaper plan, or a retention offer, and we may ask you (optionally) why you are cancelling. We will not use manipulative or deceptive design practices to obstruct or discourage cancellation, and you can always complete your cancellation.
